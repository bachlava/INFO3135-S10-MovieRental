Welcome to our Art Gallery!

ESB Art Gallery puts on display the works of renowned artists as well as promote the handiwork of up-and-coming talents all around the world. Here, you can view art from a wide range of genres. Should a particular piece of work piques your interest, we also facilitate the sales of these works.

Our History

Founded in August of 1962, ESB was revered for it's conditions for which it kept works of art that stood the test of time. Since then, ESB's once small collection has expanded drastically, exhibiting masterpieces encompassing most eras and styles, as well as new works from artists local and abroad alike.

Beautiful Grounds, and Breathtaking works

The grounds at ESB span over 50,000 square meters at the heart of the beautiful Arch City, and is nested beside the bank of the Hamsed river. Many other cultural sights await you in the illustrious capital of Arch City. If youre planning your next trip to Arch City, make sure to check out the world famous ESB museum of art history.
